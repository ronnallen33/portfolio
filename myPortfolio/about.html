About Me

I am a dedicated and detail-oriented web developer with a strong foundation in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and PHP. With over a decade of experience, I’ve built responsive, accessible, and performance-optimized websites across various platforms. My recent work at Notified Digital Media Inc. involved developing and maintaining corporate and investor-facing websites using Drupal, a powerful content management system (CMS). I specialize in creating custom themes, implementing interactive features, and ensuring compliance with brand and accessibility standards. I take pride in clean, efficient code and always strive to enhance the user experience.

My technical expertise includes structured programming with SQL, PHP, and JavaScript, along with hands-on experience using MS SQL, MySQL, and SQLite for data handling and integration. I have also worked extensively with content management systems like Drupal and WordPress, enabling fast and scalable site deployments. My toolset includes Microsoft SSIS for ETL operations, Cordova PhoneGap for mobile app development, and Adobe Photoshop for front-end design. With a solid grasp of HTML5, CSS3, Bootstrap, and CMS workflows, I can confidently manage end-to-end web projects—from UI/UX design to backend integration and server performance optimization.

I earned my degree in Information Technology and have attended extensive trainings, including Laravel Framework, SAP ABAP, Six Sigma Yellow Belt, and advanced web development courses. These have further strengthened my development, analytical, and problem-solving skills. Outside of work, I enjoy playing guitar, doing visual arts, and watching documentaries, which help fuel my creativity and attention to detail. I’m passionate about web technologies and always open to contributing to innovative teams that value quality, collaboration, and continuous growth.
