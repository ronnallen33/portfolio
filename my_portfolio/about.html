About Me

I am a dedicated web developer with over a decade of experience building responsive, accessible, and performance-optimized websites using HTML, CSS, JavaScript, PHP, and CMS platforms like Drupal and WordPress. I specialize in creating custom themes, implementing interactive features, and ensuring brand and accessibility compliance while writing clean, efficient code focused on enhancing user experience.

My expertise includes structured programming with SQL, PHP, and JavaScript, along with hands-on experience using MS SQL, MySQL, and SQLite for data integration. I’ve worked extensively with CMS platforms, enabling fast and scalable deployments, and my toolset also covers ETL operations with SSIS, mobile app development using Cordova, and front-end design with Adobe Photoshop. With a strong grasp of HTML5, CSS3, Bootstrap, and CMS workflows, I can confidently manage full project lifecycles—from UI/UX design to backend integration and server optimization.

I hold a degree in Information Technology and have completed advanced training in Laravel, SAP ABAP, Six Sigma, and modern web development practices. Outside of work, I enjoy playing guitar, exploring visual arts, and watching documentaries, all of which fuel my creativity and attention to detail. I’m passionate about technology and always eager to contribute to innovative teams that value quality, collaboration, and continuous growth.
